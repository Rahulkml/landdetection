About The System

This is an application to determine the eligibility of each and every person who wishes to buy land against their name. This system is intended to eliminate the possibilities of fraud coming under land purchase.

Our Services


Land security is a principle and critical factor in providing social and political stability. Secure land is one of mankindâ€™s most basic needs, and is a major contributor to the economic and cultural development of civilizations. To enhance land security and property ownership, research has been growing.

We provide a user friendly portal for the public to access all the land legislation laws and informations related to Kerala Land reforms act.

Enquiries related to the department can be submitted through our portal. Along with that the users can comment their feedbacks.

We provide an additional feature of digitalized cadastral map. A cadastral map is a comprehensive land recording of the real estate or real property's metes-and-bounds of a country. The main objective of this portal is to check the eligibility of every individual who is trying to buy land and hence prevent fraud, afford facility for ascertaining whether a property is governments surplus land or not.

Details regarding owned land, ration card and members list can be viewed by the officials through this portal. Eligibility to buy land can be checked through the portal in a secure manner.
